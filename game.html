Hello. This is my Mad Libs game for my CSC 106 class. This game was created with to help teach young children diffferent colors, animals, and items. The game has a start screen, an instruction screen, and a choose your words screen. When you get to the choose your words screen, choose the options left from right. Once you have chosen your three words from the three different categories click MAD LIB. You will then be able to read the poem you created.
